Data Collection & Privacy

Kori Voca is designed with your privacy in mind. This app operates primarily offline and does not collect or transmit personal information to external servers. All learning progress, vocabulary data, and user preferences are stored locally on your device. Your Korean language learning journey remains completely private and under your control.
Info

Local Storage

All app data including your learning progress, character preferences, streak counters, and vocabulary mastery levels are stored locally using your device's secure storage (AsyncStorage). This data is not encrypted separately but is protected by your device's security measures. Uninstalling the app or clearing app data will permanently delete all stored information.
Info

Third-Party Services

Kori Voca does not share any personal data with third parties. The app functions entirely offline and does not require an internet connection for core features. No analytics services, advertising networks, or tracking tools are implemented. Your vocabulary learning data, achievements, and progress remain completely private.
Info

Data Backup & Recovery

All learning data is stored locally on your device without any backup functionality. In case of device loss, app deletion, or data corruption, all progress will be permanently lost. We do not provide cloud backup, data export, or recovery services to maintain your complete privacy. The developer cannot restore or recover any lost data as no copies are maintained externally.
Info

Children's Privacy

Kori Voca is suitable for learners of all ages. Since the app does not collect any personal information or require account creation, it is safe for children to use. Parents and guardians can be assured that no data about their children's usage is collected, stored externally, or shared with any parties.
Info

Contact & Support

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or the app's data practices, please feel free to contact us. We are committed to maintaining your privacy and providing a safe, effective Korean language learning experience. This privacy policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in our practices or for operational, legal, or regulatory reasons.
